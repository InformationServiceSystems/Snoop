Dr. Kate Forster and architect Alex Wyler begin a mysterious correspondence through the mailbox of a lake house, discovering that they are living two years apart. Despite their efforts to meet, a tragic accident and a misunderstanding prevent them from connecting. Eventually, Kate learns that Alex passed away two years earlier and rushes to write a letter, urging him to wait before seeking her. In a touching conclusion, they reunite at the lake house, overcoming the constraints of time.
