Sophia Howard, grieving the loss of her son, seeks the help of occultist Joseph Solomon to perform a grueling ritual to summon her guardian angel. As they undergo months of punishing exercises, Sophia harbors a desire for revenge against her son's killer, which strains their relationship. However, after Solomon's accidental death, Sophia continues the ritual alone and encounters menacing forces in the house. In the end, she finds forgiveness and receives the blessing of her guardian angel, ultimately finding peace and performing a burial for Solomon before moving on.
